 VISION



Her vision is to become an Artistic Technician, seamlessly merging technology and creativity to develop AI-driven expressive tools. She aims to harness generative AI as a collaborator, crafting interactive experiences that inspire and enhance human creativity. Rather than focusing solely on technical research, she is drawn to practical, impactful applications where technology intersects with art, design, and innovation. She envisions developing human-centered AI that expands sensory experiences, allowing anyone to express their creativity freely. Through research in Generative and practical applications in design, UI/UX, art, she strives to create intuitive, immersive tools that foster artistic exploration and enrich the creative process.
